MT4's adaptability makes it possible to trade from any device thanks to the mobile and web versions of the platform. Manage your trades and monitor market changes in real time, wherever you are.
Risk management
The platform provides risk management functions, including setting stop losses and take profits. This helps to minimize losses and lock in profits at the right time.
